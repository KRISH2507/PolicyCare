POLICY DOCUMENT
===============
Policy Name:   ActiveCover Premier
Insurer:       VitalShield Health Insurance Ltd.
Policy Type:   Individual Comprehensive Health + Wellness Plan

---

OVERVIEW
--------
ActiveCover Premier is a premium health insurance plan designed for health-conscious
urban professionals, athletes, and high-income individuals who want comprehensive
inpatient coverage combined with robust OPD, wellness, and preventive care benefits.
It is the most feature-rich plan in the VitalShield portfolio and is optimised for
metro city residents with active lifestyles.

---

PREMIUM & COVER
---------------
Annual Premium:  ₹28,000 (age 18–35) | ₹36,000 (age 36–50) | ₹48,000 (age 51–60)
Sum Insured:     ₹15,00,000 (Fifteen Lakh Rupees)
Restore Benefit: Sum insured automatically restored once per policy year if exhausted
                 (for unrelated illness/injury)
Policy Term:     1 year, renewable annually
Grace Period:    30 days from renewal due date

---

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
Minimum Entry Age:  18 years
Maximum Entry Age:  60 years
Renewability:       Lifetime

---

INCLUSIONS (WHAT IS COVERED)
-----------------------------
Inpatient & Hospitalisation:
  - Inpatient hospitalisation for illness or accidental injury (minimum 24-hour admission)
  - Pre-hospitalisation: 90 days before admission
  - Post-hospitalisation: 180 days after discharge
  - All 540 IRDAI-listed day-care procedures
  - Domiciliary hospitalisation: covered (minimum 3 days)
  - Organ donor expenses: up to ₹3,00,000

OPD (Outpatient Department) — Key Differentiator:
  - OPD consultations: up to ₹15,000/year (any registered doctor, no network restriction)
  - Diagnostic tests (OPD): up to ₹10,000/year
  - Pharmacy (OPD prescriptions): up to ₹8,000/year
  - Physiotherapy: up to ₹12,000/year (sports injuries, post-surgical rehab)
  - Dental OPD: up to ₹5,000/year (routine check-ups and fillings)
  - Vision OPD: up to ₹4,000/year (spectacles, contact lenses, eye tests)

Sports & Fitness:
  - Sports injury treatment: covered under inpatient and OPD (no exclusion for
    amateur or professional sports activities)
  - Gym membership reimbursement: up to ₹6,000/year (NABH-accredited fitness centres)
  - Nutritionist consultation: up to ₹3,000/year
  - Annual comprehensive health check-up: full panel (CBC, lipid, HbA1c, thyroid,
    ECG, chest X-ray, abdominal ultrasound) — covered once per year

Mental Health:
  - Inpatient psychiatric care: covered (as per IRDAI mandate)
  - Outpatient mental health consultations: up to ₹8,000/year
  - Stress management programme: covered via empanelled digital wellness platform

Maternity (included, no rider required):
  - Normal delivery: up to ₹60,000
  - Caesarean delivery: up to ₹90,000
  - Newborn cover from Day 1 (up to ₹50,000 for newborn hospitalisation)
  - Maternity waiting period: 2 years from policy inception

International Emergency Cover:
  - Emergency hospitalisation outside India: up to USD 25,000 per event
  - Medical evacuation: up to USD 10,000

---

EXCLUSIONS (WHAT IS NOT COVERED)
----------------------------------
- Initial waiting period: 30 days (all illnesses except accidents)
- Cosmetic or aesthetic surgery (unless reconstructive post-accident)
- Self-inflicted injuries, substance abuse
- War, nuclear, biological, or chemical hazard events
- Experimental treatments not approved by ICMR or equivalent body
- Obesity treatment and bariatric surgery (unless BMI > 40 with life-threatening
  comorbidities, subject to medical board approval)
- Infertility treatment beyond 2 IVF cycles (covered up to ₹1,00,000 total)

---

WAITING PERIODS
---------------
Initial Waiting Period:          30 days (all illnesses except accidents)
Pre-existing Diseases:           1 year (shortest in class)
Specific Disease Waiting Period: 1 year (cataracts, hernia, joint replacement)
Maternity:                       2 years

Note: The 1-year PED waiting period is among the shortest available in the Indian
market for a comprehensive plan. Ideal for individuals with recently diagnosed
conditions who need coverage quickly.

---

CO-PAY
------
No co-pay for policyholders below age 55.
10% co-pay for policyholders aged 55–60.
No city-based co-pay (metro or otherwise).

---

ROOM RENT
---------
Single private AC room: No sub-limit (actuals covered up to sum insured).
ICU: No sub-limit.
No proportionate deduction clause.

---

SUB-LIMITS
----------
No sub-limits on surgical procedures.
OPD sub-limits as listed in inclusions section.
Maternity sub-limits as listed in inclusions section.

---

CLAIM TYPE
----------
Cashless: Available at 12,000+ network hospitals across India, including all major
metro hospitals, specialty cancer centres, cardiac institutes, and sports medicine
facilities.
Reimbursement: Submit original bills within 45 days of discharge.
Claim settlement target: 98% of cashless claims pre-authorised within 1 hour.
Dedicated relationship manager assigned for claims above ₹5 lakh.

---

WELLNESS & DIGITAL BENEFITS
----------------------------
- VitalShield Health App: track OPD claims, book teleconsultations, view policy
- Teleconsultation: unlimited (24x7 via app)
- Wearable integration: Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin — activity data linked for
  premium discount calculation
- Premium discount on renewal: up to 10% for maintaining healthy activity scores
  (10,000 steps/day average, verified via app)
- Annual wellness score report: personalised health risk assessment

---

NO-CLAIM BONUS
--------------
15% increase in sum insured for each claim-free year, up to a maximum of 100%
(sum insured can double to ₹30 lakh over time).
Bonus reduced by 15% (not reset) if a claim is made.
OPD claims do not affect no-claim bonus.

---

SUITABILITY SUMMARY
--------------------
Best suited for:
  - Metro city residents (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune)
    with high hospital cost exposure
  - Athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and active professionals who need OPD and
    sports injury coverage
  - High-income individuals (₹15 lakh+ annual income) who want premium-tier
    coverage with no sub-limits
  - Young professionals planning families (maternity included without a rider)
  - Individuals who want the shortest possible PED waiting period (1 year)

Not recommended for:
  - Budget-conscious buyers (premium is 2–4x higher than basic plans)
  - Tier-2 or Tier-3 city residents who may not utilise metro-focused benefits
  - Individuals above 60 (maximum entry age is 60)
