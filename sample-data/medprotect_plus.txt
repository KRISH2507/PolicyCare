POLICY DOCUMENT
===============
Policy Name:   MedProtect Plus
Insurer:       HealthFirst Insurance Ltd.
Policy Type:   Individual & Family Floater Health Insurance
Document Ref:  HF-MPP-2024-v2

---

OVERVIEW
--------
MedProtect Plus is a mid-segment comprehensive health insurance plan built for
working professionals and families managing chronic conditions such as diabetes,
hypertension, and asthma. It offers a shorter pre-existing disease waiting period
than most comparable plans, making it the preferred choice for individuals who
need meaningful coverage sooner.

---

PREMIUM & COVER
---------------
Individual Plan:
  Annual Premium:  ₹12,000 (age 18–40) | ₹17,500 (age 41–55) | ₹24,000 (age 56–65)
  Sum Insured:     ₹5,00,000 (Five Lakh Rupees)

Family Floater (2 adults + 2 children):
  Annual Premium:  ₹22,000 (primary insured age 18–40)
  Sum Insured:     ₹7,00,000 (Seven Lakh Rupees, shared across family)

Policy Term:       1 year, renewable annually
Grace Period:      30 days from renewal due date

---

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
Minimum Entry Age:  91 days (children under floater) | 18 years (individual)
Maximum Entry Age:  65 years
Renewability:       Lifetime

---

INCLUSIONS (WHAT IS COVERED)
-----------------------------
- Inpatient hospitalisation for illness or accidental injury (minimum 24-hour admission)
- Pre- and post-hospitalisation: 60 days before and 90 days after discharge
- Day-care procedures: all 540 IRDAI-listed procedures covered
- Ambulance charges: up to ₹3,000 per event (air ambulance: up to ₹1,00,000)
- Organ donor expenses: up to ₹1,50,000
- AYUSH treatment: up to ₹50,000/year at NABH-accredited facilities
- Domiciliary hospitalisation: covered if hospital admission is not possible
  (minimum 3 days of treatment at home)
- Mental health treatment: inpatient psychiatric care covered (as per IRDAI mandate)
- Diabetes management programme: annual HbA1c test, ophthalmology check, and
  podiatry consultation covered under wellness benefit (up to ₹5,000/year)
- Second medical opinion: one consultation per policy year at empanelled hospitals

---

PRE-EXISTING DISEASE (PED) COVERAGE — KEY DIFFERENTIATOR
----------------------------------------------------------
Diabetes (Type 1 and Type 2):     Covered after 2-year waiting period
Hypertension:                     Covered after 2-year waiting period
Asthma:                           Covered after 2-year waiting period
Thyroid disorders:                Covered after 2-year waiting period
Other PEDs (as declared):         Covered after 2-year waiting period

This 2-year PED waiting period is significantly shorter than the industry standard
of 3–4 years, making MedProtect Plus the recommended plan for individuals with
declared chronic conditions.

---

EXCLUSIONS (WHAT IS NOT COVERED)
----------------------------------
- Any illness in the first 30 days of policy inception (initial waiting period)
- Specific diseases in Year 1: cataracts, hernia, joint replacement, varicose veins
- Cosmetic or aesthetic surgery
- Dental treatment (unless due to accidental injury)
- Spectacles, contact lenses, hearing aids
- Maternity expenses (available as optional add-on rider at ₹3,500/year)
- Self-inflicted injuries, substance abuse
- War, nuclear, biological, or chemical hazard events
- Experimental treatments not approved by ICMR or equivalent body
- Obesity treatment and bariatric surgery (unless BMI > 40 with comorbidities,
  subject to medical board approval)

---

WAITING PERIODS
---------------
Initial Waiting Period:          30 days (all illnesses except accidents)
Pre-existing Diseases:           2 years (see PED section above)
Specific Disease Waiting Period: 1 year (cataracts, hernia, joint replacement,
                                  varicose veins)

CO-PAY
------
Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune):
  10% co-pay on all claims above ₹50,000.

Tier-2 cities: No co-pay.
Tier-3 cities: No co-pay.

Policyholders aged 60 and above: Additional 10% co-pay (cumulative with city co-pay
where applicable, maximum 20%).

---

ROOM RENT
---------
Up to ₹4,000 per day (single AC room).
ICU: Up to ₹8,000 per day.
No proportionate deduction if room rent limit is not exceeded.

---

SUB-LIMITS
----------
Cataract surgery:         ₹40,000 per eye
Knee/hip replacement:     ₹1,50,000 per joint
Dialysis (per session):   ₹2,500
Chemotherapy (per cycle): ₹50,000

---

CLAIM TYPE
----------
Cashless: Available at 8,500+ network hospitals across India, including major
metro hospitals and specialty centres.
Reimbursement: Submit original bills within 30 days of discharge.
Claim settlement target: 95% of cashless claims pre-authorised within 2 hours.

---

WELLNESS BENEFITS
-----------------
- Annual health check-up: covered once per policy year (basic panel)
- Diabetes management: HbA1c, eye check, foot check (up to ₹5,000/year)
- Teleconsultation: unlimited consultations via empanelled telemedicine platform
- Fitness tracker discount: 5% premium discount on renewal if 8,000 steps/day
  average maintained (verified via linked app)

---

NO-CLAIM BONUS
--------------
10% increase in sum insured for each claim-free year, up to a maximum of 50%.
Bonus is reduced by 10% (not reset) if a claim is made.

---

SUITABILITY SUMMARY
--------------------
Best suited for:
  - Individuals aged 25–55 with declared pre-existing conditions (diabetes,
    hypertension, asthma) who need coverage within 2 years
  - Metro and Tier-2 city residents who need higher room rent limits
  - Working professionals with ₹3–15 lakh annual income seeking comprehensive cover
  - Families wanting a floater plan with chronic disease coverage

Not recommended for:
  - Individuals seeking the lowest possible premium (CareShield Basic is cheaper)
  - Those needing maternity cover without purchasing the optional rider
  - Policyholders above 65 (maximum entry age is 65)
